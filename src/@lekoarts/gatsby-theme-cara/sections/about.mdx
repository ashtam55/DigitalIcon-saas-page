# About
Digital Icon helps you to visualize all your Digital interactions at one place.
 Digital Icon gives you the joy of enjoying watching your Digital Growth on a Physical level. 
 It is a Photo frame which connects your Digital Identity and shows you the numbers which really matters.
 For example if you are an Youtuber then you can have your subscriber count, if you are a Developer then you can show lines of commit on your github pages.
